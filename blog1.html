Hello! My name is Mike, and welcome to my blog :)

I'm not sure yet exactly what this platform is going to be yet. My initial plan when I decided to start this was to write tutorials and help explain complicated Software Engineering topics in a simpler way, that could be accessible to all audiences.

For the most part, this blog will be mostly that, and I'll refer to those posts as "Tech Guides". However, as much as I love computer science, I have other interests as well.

There will probably be some none CS related posts. Music, video games, basketball, and consumer electronics are just a handful of my interests outside of the programming spectrum. Beyond that, I love to think and ponder about various topics of life, so a few of these might just be writing my thoughts out.

This blog doesn't really have an identity yet, and that's ok. Nothing really does at inception. This is good though, as it gives me a chance to be creative and to experiment without the pressures of hitting specific goals.

The only constraint I'm going to put on myself is that I'll have a post about something out every Friday here going forward. This way you all know when to look for new content and I have a way of holding myself accountable.

Lastly, I'm going to post all of these on Medium as well, as I know many of you may prefer to read your blogs over there.

I'll be back next Friday with the first actual post.

Have a nice weekend,

Mike
