About me

Hello! My name is Michael Hendrick and I'm currently a Junior studying Computer Science at Boston University.

Next Summer (2019), I'll be a Software Engineering Intern at Facebook. Previously, I was a Software Engineering Intern at Akamai Technologies, working on a web-development project. Before that, I was a software quality assurance intern at Mindera Inc. Across these positions, I've been blessed to be surrounded by very talented developers, who have encouraged me to develop some pretty nifty projects over the years.

During the school year, I'm lucky enough to have the opportunity to be a Senior undergraduate course assistant for the BU computer Science department, where every semester I get a chance to share my passion for CS with new students. I'm also the Techinal Lead for a chapter of Hack4Impact, an oncampus organization that creates applications for non-profits in our communities. Lastly, I play an active role in organzining BostonHacks, one of the Northeast's premier hackathons.
